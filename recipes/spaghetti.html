"Spaghetti sauce recipe for when you feel like shit"

This is delicious to simmer meatballs in - add cooked meatballs in during the last 15 minutes of simmering.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried parsley
  • A half teaspoon salt
  • A quarter teaspoon dried oregano
  • A quarter teaspoon dried basil
  • A quarter teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps:

    Step 1: Combine diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, garlic powder, sugar, parsley, salt, oregano, basil, and pepper in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Lower heat to medium-low, cover saucepan, and simmer until flavors blend, about 30 minutes.
