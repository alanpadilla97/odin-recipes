Sopita calentita pa cuando pega el ofri

Ricolina sopita como la que hace la abuela muerta jaja. Se te murio la nona? NO te preocupes que aca tenes una sopita que te va a recordar cuando la tenias.

Ingredientes

    2 turkey legs 1 cup diced celery 1 ½ cups diced potatoes 2 (10.75 ounce) cans condensed cream of chicken soup 1 pound processed cheese, cubed 1 cup diced carrots 1 cup diced onion 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped broccoli 4 cups water

Pasitos a seguir

    Boil turkey in water until tender. Cut up meat, and add back into turkey broth.
    Add onions, potatoes, carrots, and celery. Boil until tender.
    Add frozen vegetables, and cook 15 minutes more.
    Stir in cream of chicken soup and cubed cheese. Cook over medium-low heat until cheese melts, stirring often.
